01 · Origin

Sound follows
footsteps.

Brooklyn Bridge → Chinatown, In the Mood for Love in my headphones. For a few minutes the city stopped being a place and became a film.

the word
flâneur
/flɑ.nœʁ/ · french, 19th c.
n. — the one who wanders the city not to arrive, but to feel it. The stroller. The watcher. The one who reads the street like a page.
the app

The song that belongs to
every street you walk.

Not a shuffle — the sound of where you're standing, rooted in each neighborhood's culture and mood.

the one rule

Music leads.
Geography follows.

It never interrupts a song. The city waits for the track to end, then changes the music — on its own time, not yours.
